younger smarter

Posted on 2020-07-18 In life

Is it true younger smarter

There are so many evidence to show that people achieve most achievement at relative young age suck as Newton, Einstein. In our daily life we also could find the younger generation know lot of new technique and knowledge with less effort than older generation such as they use more functions of mobile phone, computer etc.

Really hate to admit that I am also getting older and will inevitable be washed away by smarter and younger people. The answer is “yes”, according to normal observation and also basic biology theory that humans’ mental and physical ability will reach peak in their life period also fail down to earth at the end stage.

what can I do

first of all, face the death, don’t avoid it. This is because if people forget the end of episode of their life-death, they will lost their goal and meaning of this valuable journey. For example, the poorly emperors in Chinese Dynasty tried to be immortal and forever young with a lot of unreasonable method such as eating wild animal, herb medicine and other wire stuff. Then the science an technology wasn’t breed in that atmosphere, the rulers also didn’t extended their life by thinking and inventing.

Face the death is not escape, avoid and afraid of death is escape. When I talk about death everyone will feel bad, when I was young I also felt sad that everyone has to dead at the end. But like the real confident is accept the unconfident future, real unlimited life meaning is accept the limit life period.

OK, now face the tragedy, and ask myself what can I do.

Extend the life limit!

what is life limit

Life limit can be restricted or extended, depend how a person dependencies.

restrict life limit

1 social media, such as wechat, twitter facebook which only contains little fragment information. What’s more it is under censorship and propagandize.

2 boring, tedious, unhealthy social life, like play poke, drink alcohol, smoking, they not only waste people’s time but also hurt people’s mental and physical health.

All these activities will make people weak to be independent, and cannot resist loneliness.

It’s a paradox I said before when people addicted to easy money which benefits temporary,
they cannot make real money since they are too greedy to accumulate money as usual.

Same as money, the restriction of lift limit is tempting, when failed into it feel great but in the longer perspective it hurts more.

extend life limit

difficulties

First of all, need to declare the obstacle meaning and necessary because people always say silly things that “difficulties are great things, they make perfect”

1 No, not all difficulties are good.

Cutting one’s hand or leg are not good obstacles. If it happened then try to lower its effect.

2 inevitable difficulties are good.

The only unchangeable thing is changing all the time. The inevitable obstacle is development of humans culture, especially science and technology.
No matter who you are and how rich is, you have to face this difficulty.

This is good because it like former invention suck as highspeed train, computer, diversity people life, extend life limit.

how to extend

The real private property is one person’s knowledge and corresponding logical mindset is private and formed by accumulation.

1 treat difficulty
Relate to difficulty above, face the right difficulty is the first step, many people fail in front of exploding information area, their learning speed are slower than the word’s technique update speed. Or they use bad “shortcut” to prevent they really learn useful thing.

The second step is to benefit from difficulty, it depends how people treat difficulty.
So the difficulty won’t make people better but the treatment and attitude to difficulty

2 be independent

Why people need to stay together, and work at the same company address? because they have to cooperate together to finish task, that’s team work. right
But nowadays the technique changes a lot, people who can work at home replying internet are influenced by coronavirus less.

In the other words, who can be more independent who gets more advantages. Final stage is that they explode themselves and cutoff more dependencies on other tihngs and people.

In the human history, every technology revolution will provide better platform for people to equip themselves with new mindset and knowledge. But it’s not free and cannot be purchased. It cost people’s pain and headache to think to accumulate.

ok
I’m old but tomorrow is older, so extend life limit right now. Grab time!
